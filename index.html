Daeun Song

Assistant Professor  ·  Ewha Womans University, Korea

I am an Assistant Professor at Ewha Womans University, Korea, where I lead the Advanced Intelligent Robotics Lab (AIR Lab). Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Associate in the Robotixx group at George Mason University, USA, working with Dr. Xuesu Xiao, and at the University of Maryland with Dr. Dinesh Manocha. I completed my Ph.D. in CSE at Ewha Womans University, advised by Dr. Young J. Kim in the Computer Graphics Lab.

My work explores real-world robotic intelligence, and I am excited about a future in which robots and automated systems seamlessly integrate into our lives in useful, adaptive, and human-centered ways!
