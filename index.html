Daeun Song

I'm a postdoctoral associate at Maryland Robotics Center at University of Maryland, working with Dr. Dinesh Manocha. I recieved my Ph.D. degree at Ewha Womnas University, Korea in 2023, advised by Dr. Young J. Kim in Computer Graphics Lab.

I work on contact-related robotic motion planning problems.
